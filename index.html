Your first visit

No surprises. Here's exactly how it goes.

From the moment you walk in, you're treated like family. This is the real path from front door to feeling better.

  1. 1

    Welcome & paperwork

    Our receptionist welcomes you and gets your health history — you can fill the new-patient forms out online ahead of time to save the wait.

  2. 2

    Consultation

    You sit down with Dr. Leger to talk through your concerns, your history, and the options — a real conversation, free for new patients.

  3. 3

    Chiropractic exam

    A complete exam checks your reflexes, flexibility, posture, and more — with standard neurological and orthopedic tests, always with your consent.

  4. 4

    X-rays, if needed

    Depending on your condition, we may take X-rays to build the most effective, precise treatment plan for you.

  5. 5

    Report of findings

    Dr. Leger walks you through everything he found — the plan, how often you'll come in, and the cost. Clear and up front.

  6. 6

    Treatment

    Care may include spinal adjustments, physical therapy, and soft-tissue massage — often starting the very same day.

  7. 7

    Your wellness plan

    You leave with a plan for outside the office — ice/heat guidance, activity tips, and simple home exercises to keep you well.

A plan that goes somewhere

Three phases — from relief to real, lasting wellness

Phase 1

Relief care

First, we get you out of pain. Visits are more frequent up front to calm symptoms fast and give your body a break.

Phase 2

Corrective care

Next, we address the underlying cause so the pain doesn't just come roaring back. Muscles and tissues get time to heal fully.

Phase 3

Wellness care

Finally, occasional tune-ups keep you feeling your best and catch small issues before they turn into big ones.

Meet your chiropractor

Dr. James Leger, D.C.

For more than 25 years, Dr. Leger has cared for families across Ascension Parish and the surrounding communities. He treats adults and children alike, using Diversified, Gonstead, and Activator techniques to relieve pain and correct spinal dysfunction.
