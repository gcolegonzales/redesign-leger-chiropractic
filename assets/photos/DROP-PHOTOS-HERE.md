# Drop real photos here

This redesign is built to use **real photos of Dr. Leger and the office** the moment
they're available. The current live site (legerchiropractic.com) only ships a tiny
generic ChiroMatrix template banner and demo stock imagery — nothing real of the
doctor or the practice — so no owner photo could be pulled. Those slots use a tasteful
designed placeholder for now, marked `<!-- IMG-NEEDED -->` in `index.html`.

- Natural light, neutral/warm tones so they sit well with the sand + pine palette.

Until then the site still looks intentional and complete.
